The Manifold of Completed Intelligibility

A systematic account of the finite forms through which intelligibility becomes complete, survives, and enters later thought.

The project proposes that every properly individuated completed intelligibility lands through one of fifty-four event-forms generated by six work-families, three settlement orientations, and three survivor-modes.
